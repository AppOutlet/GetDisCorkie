This Privacy Policy explains how DisCorkie ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"), developed by AppOutlet, collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile and desktop application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information You Provide Directly:
    • Email Address: When you create an account and log in using our passwordless authentication method, you provide your email address.
  • Information Collected Automatically:
    • Unique User Identifier (UUID): Assigned by Supabase to manage your account and subscription status.
    • Discourse Authentication Tokens: Stored only on your device and used to access your Discourse accounts. Deleted when you sign out.
    • Usage Data and Crash Reports: Collected anonymously to improve performance. Does not personally identify you.

Please note that we do not store or collect your password for your DisCorkie account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage your account and features.
  • To enable secure interaction with Discourse forums via local tokens.
  • To manage and sync subscriptions across platforms.
  • To monitor performance, fix bugs, and improve user experience.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Authentication Services: We use Supabase for passwordless login and account management.
  • Subscription Management: We use Adapty, which interacts with Google, Apple, and Stripe.
  • Discourse Instances: Your interactions with third-party Discourse forums are governed by their own policies.
  • Analytics and Crash Reporting: We use Firebase for anonymized data and crash reports.
  • Legal Requirements and Protection: We may disclose data if legally required or to protect rights and safety.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your email and UUID are stored securely with Supabase. Authentication tokens are kept only on your device. We use encryption and secure protocols where appropriate.

5. Data Retention

We keep account data while your account is active. If deleted, it is removed from active databases but may persist in backups temporarily. Local tokens stay on your device until you sign out or uninstall the App.
