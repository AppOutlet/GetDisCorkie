Join DisCorkie’s Android Early Access – Be Among the First to Try It!

By AppOutlet team
21 March 2025

We’re excited to announce that DisCorkie—the ultimate Discourse client for discovering and managing forums—is almost ready for launch on Android! But before we officially release it, we need 12 enthusiastic testers to help us fine-tune the experience.

Why Join Early Access?

🚀 Get Exclusive First Access – Be among the first to try DisCorkie on Android before anyone else.

🐾 Help Shape the App – Your feedback will directly influence improvements and new features.

🔎 Discover & Manage with Ease – Seamlessly explore and organize multiple Discourse communities in one place.

💬 Engage in Discussions Smoothly – Enjoy an intuitive interface designed for effortless navigation and interaction.

How to Join?

To apply, send an email to team.appoutlet@gmail.com with the subject “Android Early Access.” Once selected, you’ll receive further instructions on how to download and test the app.

Spots are limited, so sign up now and be part of DisCorkie’s launch journey!

Questions? Reach out to us at team.appoutlet@gmail.com. We can’t wait to have you on board! 🚀
