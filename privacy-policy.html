Your DisCorkie account information (email and UUID) is stored securely with our authentication provider, Supabase. We take reasonable measures to protect the information we handle, including using encryption and secure communication protocols where appropriate.

Importantly, the authentication tokens for the Discourse instances you add are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your DisCorkie account information (email and UUID) for as long as your account is active. Locally stored Discourse authentication tokens are retained on your device until you sign out of the specific Discourse instance within DisCorkie or uninstall the App.

Anonymized usage data and crash reports are retained for as long as necessary to analyze trends and improve the App.
