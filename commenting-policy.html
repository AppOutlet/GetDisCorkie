DisCorkie Blog Commenting Policy

By AppOutlet team
21 March 2025

We welcome open discussions and value your feedback! To keep this space respectful and engaging for everyone, please follow these guidelines when commenting:

Be Respectful

  • Treat others with kindness and respect. Disagreements are natural, but personal attacks, harassment, or hostility will not be tolerated.

Zero Tolerance for Discrimination

  • Any form of discrimination, hate speech, or offensive remarks based on race, gender, religion, nationality, or any other personal characteristic will result in a permanent ban.

Stay on Topic

  • Keep comments relevant to the post. Off-topic discussions, spam, or self-promotion may be removed.

No Misbehavior

  • Threats, trolling, doxxing, or any form of disruptive behavior will lead to immediate account suspension.

Moderation & Enforcement

  • We reserve the right to remove any comments that violate these guidelines. Users who repeatedly break the rules will be permanently banned from commenting.
